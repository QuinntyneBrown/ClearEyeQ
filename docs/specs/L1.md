# ClearEyeQ — L1 Requirements (Design)

## L1-0: User-Facing Applications
The platform shall consist of three user-facing applications:
1. **Patient Mobile App** — built with React Native + Expo, targeting iOS and Android. Provides eye scan capture, health dashboard, timeline, diagnosis viewing, treatment tracking, notifications, and settings.
2. **Clinical Portal** — built with React + TypeScript (Next.js), targeting desktop web browsers. Provides patient list, longitudinal health data, AI diagnostic summaries, treatment review/approval, referral management, and clinical notes.
3. **Admin Portal** — built with Blazor (Server or WebAssembly), targeting desktop web browsers. Provides tenant management, subscription oversight, system monitoring, feature flag management, and operational tooling.

## L1-1: Onboarding & Authentication Screens
The platform shall provide responsive onboarding and authentication flows including splash/welcome, sign-up, login, and account setup screens across all breakpoints (XS, SM, MD, LG).

## L1-2: Dashboard & Home Screen
The platform shall provide a main dashboard/home screen showing eye health score, recent scan summary, environmental context cards, and quick-action buttons, responsive across all breakpoints.

## L1-3: Smart Eye Scan Flow
The platform shall provide a guided eye scan capture experience with camera viewfinder, AI-guided positioning feedback, scan progress, and results display, responsive across all breakpoints.

## L1-4: Eye Health Timeline & Trends
The platform shall provide a timeline/trends view showing historical redness scores, environmental correlation charts, and pattern insights, responsive across all breakpoints.

## L1-5: Diagnosis & Root Cause Report
The platform shall provide diagnosis screens showing differential diagnosis results, confidence scores, causal graphs, and condition detail cards, responsive across all breakpoints.

## L1-6: Treatment & Action Plans
The platform shall provide treatment plan screens showing active protocols, medication/behavioral interventions, dosing schedules, and progress tracking, responsive across all breakpoints.

## L1-7: Predictive Alerts & Notifications
The platform shall provide notification and alert components including 72-hour forecast banners, flare-up alerts, treatment reminders, and specialist referral prompts, responsive across all breakpoints.

## L1-8: Settings, Profile & Data Management
The platform shall provide settings and profile screens including personal info, connected devices/apps, privacy controls, data export, subscription management, and accessibility options, responsive across all breakpoints.

## L1-9: Shared UI Components & Design System
The platform shall provide a comprehensive set of reusable UI components — buttons (all states), dialogs/modals, toast notifications, bottom sheets, navigation bars, tab bars, cards, form inputs, and loading states — designed responsively across all breakpoints.

## L1-10: Clinical Partner Portal
The platform shall provide a clinician-facing portal with patient list, longitudinal patient eye health data, AI diagnostic summaries, and referral management, responsive across all breakpoints.
